About Pat

  • Name: Pat Di Orio
  • Height: 5'11"
  • Jacket Size: 43
  • Hair Color: Dark Brown
  • Eyes Color: Brown

Pat Di Orio was born in Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada and raised in Ottawa, Ontario;living in both the city and the country, he’s definitely had a taste of both worlds.

Among his favorite things growing up were to play video games. He remembers getting his first Nintendo 64 system and losing his mind, playing endless hours of his favorite games. The stage was also a joyful venture he felt drawn to. He remembers when he was 10, dropping his KinglyCrown, the first time he ever spoke his lines in from of an audience, and quickly scrambling topick it up and place it back on his head, as the audience roared in laughter.

Some unusual accomplishments in his life are winning the ‘Top Drill Commander’ at Air Cadet camp, during the summer of 1998; Winning the most determined student award at his High School graduation, which totally came as a surprise and shock to him; Winning his city regional football championship in 2004. He also dressed himself as a woman for a student film in the middle of Columbus Circle, NYC.

His most proud accomplishments are, graduating from a two year acting conservatory in NYC (May 2015), making a student short film called The Godson, based on the classic movie The Godfather, and loving life, while getting better everyday.

French is his first language, yet he’s also fluent in English, and knows Italian pretty well. He enjoys working out, playing sports, football, tennis, basketball, playing PC games, watching his favorite TV shows. His favorite thing to do on Sundays is watch the NFL. Pat is a huge die-hard Los Angeles Rams fan, and one day will have season tickets for life.

He has a surprisingly spot on Arnold Schwarzenegger impersonation, also capable of doing Sylvester Stallone and Sean Connery.

Pat currently lives in New York City. It’s his new home away from home and he loves the city and he wouldn’t want it any other way.
