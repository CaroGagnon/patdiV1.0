Pat Di Iorio was born in Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada and raised in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. His parents are both lawyers, which is something Pat did not want to do!

His favorite things growing up were to play video games. He remembers getting his first Nintendo 64 system and losing his mind, playing endless hours of his favorite games.

Some unusual accomplishments in his life are winning the ‘Top Drill Commander’ at Air Cadet camp, during the summer of 1998; Winning the most determined student award at his High School graduation, which totally came as a surprise and shock to him; Winning his city regional football championship in 2004. He once dressed as a woman for a student film in the middle of Columbus Circle, NYC.

French is his first language, yet is also fluent in English, and knows Italian pretty well.

He enjoys playing sports, Football, Tennis. He’s also a PC gamer, Fallout 4, shooters like Call of Duty series (for his inner competitiveness). His favorite thing to do on Sundays is watch the NFL. Pat is a huge die-hard Los Angeles Rams fan.

He has a surprisingly spot on Arnold Schwarzenegger impersonation, also capable of doing Sylvester Stallone and Sean Connery.

Pat currently lives in New York City. It’s his new home away from home and he loves the city.
